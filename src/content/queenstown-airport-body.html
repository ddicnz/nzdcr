Pick up information

Our Queenstown Airport branch is located just minutes away from the terminal.

Pick up details

Upon arrival at the airport, after you have collected your luggage and cleared customs, please contact our customer service by calling 820 using the free phone located at the TRANSFER area (or dial 09 275 4085) and make your way to the TRANSFERS point. Our shuttle is expected to arrive within approximately 10 minutes at the TRANSFERS location.

If you prefer to drive directly to our office, you are welcome to visit us at 121 Glenda Drive, Frankton, Queenstown.

Drop off information

At the expiry of your rental agreement please return your vehicle back to our office at 121 Glenda Drive. We will have one of our drivers take you on to the airport.

Do not leave your vehicle at the airport.
