We are allied with one of Auckland’s biggest, oldest and most respected used car importers, Mike Vinsen Motors, who have been an important part of the New Zealand car industry for over 36 years. Our NZ car rentals have been operating as a separate company since 2007; however, we have held a rental license since 1980.

Our vehicles are of the highest quality

All of our vehicles are of the highest quality and have undergone comprehensive NZ Land Transport Safety Authority checks. We are licensed by the NZ Land Transport Safety Authority as a rental operator and we also have associate rental companies in Rarotonga, Island Car & Bike Hire and Rarotonga Rentals.

NZ Discount Car Rentals is a member of the Rental Vehicle Association of New Zealand. The Association’s Code of Ethics seeks to maintain the standards of ethical trading of members by imposing a Code of Conduct on its members in respect of safety, service and advertising.

We are friendly, efficient and flexible

If you decide a different class of vehicle is more suitable, we can change your vehicle with the minimum of paperwork and time wasted. Please feel free to make any enquiries or requests on the enquiry form located on our contact us page.

We are open 7 days a week, and can make your rental vehicle available to pick up 24/7.
