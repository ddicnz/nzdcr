New Zealand daylight saving time

When do the clocks go back one hour? When do the clocks go forward one hour?

Daylight saving in New Zealand starts on the last Sunday in September, when 2.00am becomes 3.00am, and ends on the first Sunday in April the following year, when 3.00am becomes 2.00am.

2026

Daylight saving ends on Sunday 5 April (clocks go back one hour at 3.00am to 2.00am).

Daylight saving starts on Sunday 27 September (clocks go forward one hour at 2.00am to 3.00am).
