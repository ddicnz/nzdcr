Use of Information:

NZ Discount Car Rentals LTD only requests contact information when required to respond to customer inquiries and support requests. NZ Discount Car Rentals LTD will only use information provided by users and customers for the purposes for which it was collected.

Cookies:

A cookie will be stored on a user’s computer when our website is accessed. This cookie is used to synchronize interactions between the user’s web browser and our website. The cookie does not monitor the number of times or when a user or customer visits our site, nor is it used to gather any user information. Internet browsers usually have a facility that allows a user to accept or decline a cookie (refer to the software provider’s reference material for more information).

Disclosure:

NZ Discount Car Rentals LTD will not disclose personal information to a third party without the informed consent of the individual. We will ensure that the personal information of users and customers will not be disclosed to any other institutions or authorities except if required to by law other regulation.

Information Collection:

When visiting this website, the NZ Discount Car Rentals LTD server logs the following user information that is provided by the user’s browser for statistical purposes only:
The type of browser and operating system that the user is using.
The user’s top level domain name (for example .com, .net, .gov, etc.).
The address of the referring site (for example, the previous site the user visited).
The user’s server’s IP address (a number that is unique to the machine through which the user is connected to the Internet: usually one of the user’s service provider’s machines).
The date and time of the user’s visit.
The address of the pages accessed and the documents downloaded.
The above information is used only for statistical analysis and systems administration purposes. No attempt will be made to identify users or their browsing activities, except in the unlikely event of an investigation where a law enforcement agency may exercise a warrant.

Email Details:

NZ Discount Car Rentals LTD will only record an individual’s email address if they send us an email message. It will only be used for the purposes for which the individual provides it, and will not be added to a mailing list unless the individual requests that this is done. NZ Discount Car Rentals LTD will not disclose it without the individual’s consent.

Information Security: Your card payment information is protected. Discount rentals must be secured using a valid Visa, Mastercard, etc cards

Record Deletion:

NZ Discount Car Rentals LTD will remove personal information from its systems when it is no longer required (except in the case of archiving).
